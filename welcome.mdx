**Autoppia is a decentralized marketplace for AI Workers** that connects developers and users through a flexible, framework-agnostic, privacy-preserving platform.

Autoppia allows **Developers** to build **AI Workers Templates** and *publish* them on the Marketplace to then be *discovered, customized and deployed* by **Users**.

Unleash the **full potential of AI Workers** with Autoppia **to act, not just think**!
